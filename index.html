EXPLORING AUSTRALIA'S FORESTS

Understanding the Data Behind the Forests and Plantations in Australian States

This collection of visualizations explores the distribution and evolution of plantation areas across Australia...

This choropleth map shows the sales and service income for each state.

This time series chart tracks the growth of softwood and hardwood plantations over time.

This stacked area chart shows the volume of logs harvested in each state over the years.

This bubble chart compares log volume, log value, and forest area across states.

This donut chart shows each state's contribution to the total value of logs harvested.
